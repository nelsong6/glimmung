Borders — one signature, four parts
Glimmung has one border vocabulary. Every active surface — buttons (.gb), pills (.pill), panels (.plate) — is built from the same four parts: a chamfered top-left, a 2px leading rail in a state color, a 1px hairline tracing the rest of the edge, and (where it applies) a tinted variant of the hairline to signal a running state.
